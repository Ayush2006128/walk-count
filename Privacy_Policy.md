# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** December 17, 2025

WalkCount ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our WalkCount Android application collects, uses, and safeguards your information.

## 1. Information Collection and Use

### Physical Activity Data
The App uses the `ACTIVITY_RECOGNITION` permission to access your device's built-in step counter sensor. This sensor data is used solely to:
*   Calculate and display your daily step count.
*   Track your progress towards the daily goals you set within the App.

This processing happens entirely locally on your device in real-time.

### User Preferences
We store your daily step goal locally on your device using Android's DataStore Preferences. This allows the App to remember your goal between sessions.

## 2. Data Storage and Sharing

**No Data is Uploaded:**
WalkCount is an offline-first application. We do **not** collect, transmit, or store your personal data, step counts, or usage habits on any external servers. All data remains strictly on your device.

**Third-Party Sharing:**
Since we do not collect any personal data, we do not share, sell, or disclose your information to any third parties.

## 3. Permissions

To function as intended, the App requires the following permissions:

*   **Physical Activity (`ACTIVITY_RECOGNITION`):** Required to access the step sensor.
*   **Foreground Service (`FOREGROUND_SERVICE`, `FOREGROUND_SERVICE_HEALTH`):** Required to count steps accurately while the App is running in the background.
*   **Notifications (`POST_NOTIFICATIONS`):** Required to show a persistent notification with your current step count while the service is running.
